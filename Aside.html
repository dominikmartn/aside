Aside 1.5.7

Tooltips, round two: quieter and consistent.

Changed

  • One tooltip system. Every tooltip now uses the same style. Action buttons show theirs immediately; explanatory hints (like the transcript's "Play from…" or the tag field) wait a moment, the way macOS tooltips do — but without the sluggish native delay.
  • Fewer tooltips. The × on the notch pill and the Record button in the Aside window no longer show one.
  • The Agents chip is just an indicator now. While an agent reads your live transcript, the chip shows it — clicking it no longer switches agent access off. That control lives in Settings, where an accidental click can't reach it.
