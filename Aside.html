Aside 1.3.8

A reliability release: recordings, summaries, and your audio are a lot harder to lose.

Fixed

  • Meetings that recorded but never got a summary. A settings quirk could quietly wipe the copilot connection, so notes came back blank. Summaries generate reliably now.
  • A crashed recording no longer loses its audio. Each session keeps its own recovery copy, so starting a new meeting can't delete the one you still need to recover.
  • Recording stops when you hang up. Ending a call you started recording from now stops it on its own again, instead of capturing the empty room afterward.
  • Your mic survives a device switch. Swapping to AirPods (or any input) mid-meeting no longer silently kills your side of the recording; the same for the other participants when your output device changes.
  • A crash when your audio device changed in the middle of a recording.
  • If saving a note ever fails, you now see it in the window with a Retry, instead of the meeting quietly disappearing.
  • Editing a meeting's title or summary works again.
  • Search no longer skips or duplicates rows.
  • Declining the microphone during first-run setup no longer leaves you stuck on that screen.
  • Microphone and calendar access now work on a fresh Mac.
  • A meeting you already recorded no longer keeps popping back up at the notch.

Improved

  • Overlapping calendar events are titled after the call you're actually in, not a longer background block.
  • Start recording with no copilot set up and Aside tells you up front. You still get the transcript, just no written summary until you add one.
  • When a summary can't be written, Aside now tells you why (and lets you try again) instead of a generic message.
  • First launch shows the speech model downloading with a progress bar, instead of a silent wait.

If you're new here

  • No API key needed. Aside Cloud is the default: download, open, and it works. You can still use your own provider in Settings. (Cloud is invite-only while in testing, so a new install asks for an invite code once.)
  • The Aside window is home. Every meeting note, search, and tags in one place, plus what's next on your calendar with one click to join or record. Open it with ⌘⌥A or from the menu bar.
  • Keep it open during a meeting. Pin the panel so it stays up while you work, then click the ✕ to close it.
  • Ask questions across all your past meetings, not just the one you have open.

Anonymous usage counts (never your content) help guide what gets built next, with an off switch in Settings. Crash reports can be shared too, opt-in and technical only, never audio or transcripts.
