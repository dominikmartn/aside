Aside 1.3.2

New

  • Aside Cloud is invite-only for now: new installs enter an invite code during onboarding. Existing installs keep working, nothing to do.
  • Anonymous usage stats (counts only, never content) so development follows real use. Off switch in Settings.
  • No API key needed anymore. Aside Cloud is the new default: download, open, it works. Your own key still fits any provider in Settings.
  • The Aside window is the new home. All your meeting notes, search, tags, and what's next on your calendar, one click to join or record. Open it with ⌘⌥A or from the menu bar.
  • First launch now shows the speech-model download with a progress bar instead of a silent wait.
  • Ask questions across all your past meetings, not just the open one.

Improved

  • A full design pass: new icons, calmer hover effects, monochrome tags, tidier wording everywhere.
  • Meeting notes generate faster (one model call instead of two).
  • Recording survives a quick account switch in the browser instead of ending the meeting.
  • Recovered meetings now show their progress in the list while transcribing.

Fixed

  • A crash when the audio device changed mid-recording (the 9:00-meeting bug).
  • Microphone and calendar access work in release builds on fresh Macs.
  • Turning the Dock icon off no longer closes the window.
  • Opening the window no longer drops you into a text field.

Crash reports can now be shared with the developer (opt-in, technical traces only, never audio or transcripts).
