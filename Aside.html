Aside 1.3.8

New

  • No API key needed. Aside Cloud is the default now: download, open, and it works. You can still use your own provider in Settings. (Cloud is invite-only while in testing, so a new install asks for an invite code once.)
  • The Aside window is home. Every meeting note, search, and tags in one place, plus what's next on your calendar with one click to join or record. Open it with ⌘⌥A or from the menu bar.
  • Keep it open during a meeting. Pin the panel so it stays up while you work, then click the ✕ to close it.
  • Ask questions across all your past meetings, not just the one you have open.

Improved

  • Overlapping calendar events are handled right: a call inside a longer block gets titled after the call, not the block.
  • Start recording without a copilot set up and Aside now says so up front. You still get the transcript, just no written summary until you add one.
  • Meeting notes generate faster, and a recording survives a quick browser account switch instead of ending.
  • First launch shows the speech model downloading with a progress bar, instead of a silent wait.
  • A visual refresh throughout: new app icon, clearer controls, quieter interactions.

Fixed

  • A crash when your audio device changed in the middle of a recording.
  • Microphone and calendar access now work on a fresh Mac.
  • A meeting you already recorded no longer keeps popping back up at the notch.
  • Turning off the Dock icon no longer closes the window, and opening the window no longer drops your cursor into a search field.

Anonymous usage counts (never your content) help guide what gets built next, with an off switch in Settings. Crash reports can be shared too, opt-in and technical only, never audio or transcripts.
