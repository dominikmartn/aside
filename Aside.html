Aside 1.5.3

Recovering an interrupted meeting no longer gets stuck.

Fixed

  • Crash recovery can't hang anymore. If your Mac shut down mid-meeting (battery died, or a crash), recovering that recording could get stuck on “Recovering this meeting…” forever. It now recovers reliably: it falls back to a faster model when the high-accuracy one isn't fully downloaded, shows download progress instead of a silent spinner, and gives you a clear message to retry if the network stalls — rather than spinning with no end. Your recorded audio was always kept safe on disk; this makes turning it back into a note dependable.
