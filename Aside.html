Aside 1.5.6

A small polish release for tooltips.

Fixed

  • Tooltips are no longer cut off. Hovering a control at the notch (like the × on a meeting suggestion) showed a tooltip that was clipped by the notch's edge. Tooltips now render above the panel frame everywhere — at the notch, on the floating pill, in the panel, and in the Aside window.

Changed

  • Fewer tooltips. Buttons with a visible label (Join, Record, Stop) no longer repeat themselves in a tooltip. Tooltips stay where they add something: icon-only buttons and actions with non-obvious consequences.
