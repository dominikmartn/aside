Aside 1.5.9

A brand-new first run, and Aside is ready to transcribe seconds after install.

New

  • A completely new onboarding. First launch now walks you through everything in one guided flow on your desktop: permissions with a reason for each, then you say hello and watch your own words appear live before your first real meeting.
  • Speech model included. Aside now ships with a compact speech model built in, so transcription works seconds after install. The full-quality model downloads quietly in the background and takes over automatically.

Fixed

  • Permission prompts can no longer hide. System permission dialogs and System Settings always open in front during onboarding.
  • Two first-run crashes. Fixed a crash when recording without system audio and a crash in the microphone level meter.
