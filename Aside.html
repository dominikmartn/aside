Aside 1.5.4

Clean up several meetings at once, and bring your own model.

New

  • Select several meetings and delete them together. Hold ⌘ to pick meetings one by one, ⇧ to take a whole run of them, ⌘A for everything the list is currently showing. Delete with ⌘⌫ or the right-click menu — one confirmation for the batch instead of one per meeting. Filter by a tag or search first and the selection follows what you see.
  • Bring your own model. Aside Cloud stays the default and nothing changes if you're happy with it. If you'd rather run your notes through your own account, Settings now takes a key for Kimi, OpenAI, Anthropic, GLM, or a local Ollama.
